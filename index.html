How to play
1

Create roles

Add roles like Mafia, Villager, Detective — and set how many players get each one.

2

Deal roles secretly

Each player press-holds the card to reveal their secret role, then passes the phone.

3

Night phase

Everyone closes their eyes. Mafia silently picks someone to eliminate.

4

Day phase

Town debates and votes to eliminate a suspect.

5

Win condition

Town wins when all Mafia are gone. Mafia wins when they equal or outnumber Town.
